üèõÔ∏è Faction System & Shared Resources

Smart Resource Sharing

Our faction system creates meaningful cooperation while preserving individual agency:

üí∞Shared Cats (Currency)

Faction members share a common treasury. When one player earns Cats through trading or looting, it benefits the entire faction. This encourages cooperation and creates interesting economic dynamics.

üî¨Research Collaboration

Research progress is shared among faction members. Multiple players can work on different research projects simultaneously, accelerating technological advancement.

üìäFaction Reputation

Actions by faction members affect the group's standing with NPCs. This creates accountability and encourages strategic decision-making.

Faction Leadership & Management

  • Democratic Leadership: Vote on major faction decisions
  • Resource Allocation: Set budgets and spending priorities
  • Territory Control: Claim and defend faction territories
  • Diplomatic Relations: Manage relationships with other player factions

üèÜ Faction Victory Conditions

New Goals: Compete with other player factions to control trade routes, build the largest cities, or achieve technological supremacy. These meta-objectives give long-term purpose to multiplayer sessions.
