The wavefunction of an electron in a hydrogen-like atom is given by:

ψₙₗₘ(r,θ,φ) = Rₙₗ(r) · Yₗᵐ(θ,φ)

Where:

  • Rₙₗ(r) is the radial function (describing distance dependence)
  • Yₗᵐ(θ,φ) is the spherical harmonic (describing angular shape)
  • n, l, m are quantum numbers that determine the specific orbital shape
